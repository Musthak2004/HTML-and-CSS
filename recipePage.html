Easy Spaghetti Bolognese (Serves 4)

This simple 15-minute spaghetti bolognese recipe serves four and uses staples like pasta, ground beef, and canned tomatoes. The process involves sautéing aromatics, browning meat, and simmering, providing a perfect example of how to write a clear, step-by-step recipe with listed ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 250g (8.8 oz) Spaghetti
  • 1 tablespoon Olive oil
  • 1 Onion, finely chopped
  • 2 Garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g (1.1 lbs) Minced beef
  • 400g (14 oz) Tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon Dried oregano or basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti in boiling salted water for 8–10 minutes until tender; drain.
  2. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Fry chopped onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the minced beef to the pan and cook until it is completely browned.
  4. Stir in the tinned tomatoes and herbs (oregano/basil). Season with salt and pepper. Cover and let it simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  5. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti and top with grated cheese.
